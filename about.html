Script Editior and Gingerbread Organizer, A Gingerbread Christmas (2022)

Working under gingerbread artist Beatriz Muller, I joined the crew for the movie A Gingerbread Christmas premiering November 11, 2022 on Discovery+. I was tasked with going through the script, making note of all inconsistencies and providing alternative ideas for Beatriz to present at meetings. Once the script was finalized, I organized and directed a team of artists to ensure that every piece present in the script was prepared exactly as requested. I packaged all completed pieces, labeling each one with the exact scenes they belonged to.
